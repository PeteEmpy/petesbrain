## Summary

- Grant email-only access for users who need reports without full account access (requires administrative privileges)
- Download reports as tables if user has email-only access rather than full account permissions
- Manage or cancel automated reporting schedules through the Google Ads reporting interface
- Access actual performance data through the Google Ads interface rather than relying on notification emails
- Maintain clear user access protocols for account security when distributing reports
