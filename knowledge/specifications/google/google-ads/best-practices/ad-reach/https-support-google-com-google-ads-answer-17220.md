## Summary

- Use reach estimates only as general guidance for determining relative number of users
- Compare reach across different location targets to determine best areas
- Don't rely on reach estimates to predict total impressions
- Understand that reach numbers may differ from census data due to multiple factors
- Consider 'Limited reach' warnings when targeting small geographic areas

## Full Content

- Use reach estimates only as general guidance for determining relative number of users
- Compare reach across different location targets to determine best areas
- Don't rely on reach estimates to predict total impressions
- Understand that reach numbers may differ from census data due to multiple factors
- Consider 'Limited reach' warnings when targeting small geographic areas
- Use reach to decide if targeted areas provide right level of exposure
- Evaluate whether to select other areas that may provide better reach
