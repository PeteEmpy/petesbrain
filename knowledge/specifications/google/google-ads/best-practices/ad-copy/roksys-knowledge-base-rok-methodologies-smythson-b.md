## Full Content

- Check capitalization - use sentence case throughout (not lowercase)
- Reference company age consistently as '137 years' or 'over 135 years'
- Use 'Smythson' as brand name without location suffix
- Apply American English spelling systematically for US-targeted campaigns
- Implement as mandatory QA checklist items before campaign launch
- Maintain brand consistency to prevent ad revision cycles
- Document client-specific guidelines to preserve brand equity
- Apply geographic spelling localization based on target market
