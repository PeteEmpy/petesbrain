## Full Content

- Run campaigns for at least 6 weeks to give Google AI time to gather data and optimize
- Use relevant audience lists as signals to help Google AI learn faster
- Upload rich product descriptions and images to help ads stand out
- Add images with 500x500 pixels or greater resolution for CTV placement
- Ensure listed products are approved using Product issues column
- Add local product data feed to automatically enable local inventory ads
- Plan budgets early but be ready for additional sales
- Use reporting and insights to evaluate results
