## Full Content

- Always have backup contact information for Account Manager's supervisor in case of emergency unavailability
- Google Ads support team operates independently of account managers - use for immediate technical issues
- Multiple support channels available (phone, email, chat) - choose based on urgency and issue complexity
- Always request and document case numbers when contacting support for tracking and follow-up
- Keep Customer ID readily accessible for faster support resolution
- 24/7 support is available for critical issues - don't wait for account manager return
- Merchant Center, billing, and policy issues can be resolved directly through support team without account manager
- Use online chat for quickest resolution according to Google's recommendation
- Help Center links provide self-service options for common issues before contacting support
- Distinguish between strategic consultation needs (account manager) and technical support needs (support team)
- Out of office notifications include clear escalation paths - follow the provided alternative contacts
