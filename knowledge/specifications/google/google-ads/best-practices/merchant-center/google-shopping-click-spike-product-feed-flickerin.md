## Full Content

- Implement enhanced feed monitoring to catch flickering products immediately
- Review stock synchronization between e-commerce platform and Merchant Center regularly
- Check Merchant Center feed for disapproval warnings daily
- Investigate sudden click spikes (200%+) by analyzing product-level data first
- Ensure consistent stock availability for high-performing products
- Set up alerts for products being removed/re-added to feed multiple times per day
- Verify inventory system integration is stable and not causing rapid stock status changes
- Monitor individual product performance to identify patterns before they impact budget
- Review feed upload history when diagnosing unexpected campaign performance changes
- Prioritize fixing feed stability for products with proven high click volume
- Document feed flickering incidents to identify systematic issues
- Test stock synchronization thoroughly after any platform or integration updates
