## Full Content

- Ensure both website admin AND Google Ads/Analytics admin are present on call - missing either will require rescheduling
- Always complete full website backup before implementing tracking codes to prevent data loss
- Accept calendar invite within 24 hours to prevent automatic cancellation
- Have Google Meet screen sharing tested and ready before scheduled appointment time
- Review pre-call checklist thoroughly to maximize efficiency during technical implementation session
- Implementation changes are at client's own risk - Google provides no guarantees on results or spending
- Keep case ID reference for all follow-up communications
- Technical implementation sessions are hands-on - not just advisory calls
- Expect to make live code changes during the call with Google support guidance
