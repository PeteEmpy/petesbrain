## Summary

- Ensure careful coordination between account managers, technical support, and client technical teams for ECL implementation
- Maintain continuity of technical support personnel - work with same specialist who understands specific setup
- Document case numbers and ensure proper context (client ID, correct invitees) when creating support cases
- Schedule follow-up technical calls with the same specialist who provided initial guidance

- Ensure careful coordination between account managers, technical support, and client technical teams for ECL implementation
