## Summary

- Update internal records when Google account management contact changes
- Route urgent requests through designated interim contact (manager level)
- Plan for potential delays in strategic discussions during account manager absence
- Maintain documentation of primary and backup Google contacts
- Use 24/7 A+ Team Chat for technical support issues during account manager unavailability

## Full Content

- Update internal records when Google account management contact changes
- Route urgent requests through designated interim contact (manager level)
- Plan for potential delays in strategic discussions during account manager absence
- Maintain documentation of primary and backup Google contacts
- Use 24/7 A+ Team Chat for technical support issues during account manager unavailability
- Allow time for account manager to reconnect and reschedule after extended leave
- Respect bereavement periods and avoid non-urgent outreach during such times
