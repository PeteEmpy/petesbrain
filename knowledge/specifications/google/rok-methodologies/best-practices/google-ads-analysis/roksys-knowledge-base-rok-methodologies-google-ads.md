## Full Content

- Use structured prompts for repeatable analysis across different accounts and time periods
- Include comprehensive analysis areas: campaigns, products, placements, audiences, assets, budget efficiency
- Perform root cause diagnostics beyond metrics: feed quality, auction competition, creative fatigue
- Prioritize 3-5 specific actionable recommendations rather than overwhelming with data
- Flag week-over-week changes exceeding ±15% in ROAS or conversions for investigation
- Identify top 10 best-performing and bottom 10 worst-performing products by ROAS and volume
- Analyze impression share loss opportunities (budget vs. rank issues) for Shopping campaigns
- Format reports in Markdown with executive summaries, tables (max 20 rows), and bullet-pointed insights
- Use human-friendly metrics (dollars, percentages) with clear section headings
- Leverage MCP integration with Claude Code for direct Google Ads data access
- Adapt prompts for manual workflows when MCP integration unavailable
- Focus on e-commerce specific metrics: product-level ROAS, conversion tracking, placement performance
