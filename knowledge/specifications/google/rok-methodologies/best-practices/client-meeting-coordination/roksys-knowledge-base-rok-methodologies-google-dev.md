## Summary

- Use '[please confirm]' prefix in meeting titles when awaiting attendee confirmation
- Schedule meetings during business hours appropriate for all participants' timezones (e.g., 14:00 GMT for European participants)
- Provide multiple dial-in options for accessibility (Google Meet link + phone numbers)

## Full Content

- Use '[please confirm]' prefix in meeting titles when awaiting attendee confirmation
- Schedule meetings during business hours appropriate for all participants' timezones (e.g., 14:00 GMT for European participants)
- Provide multiple dial-in options for accessibility (Google Meet link + phone numbers)
- Maintain regular check-in cadence between Google partnerships and client/agency coordination
- Keep check-in meetings brief (30 minutes) for status updates rather than deep-dive working sessions
- Create calendar invites with sufficient advance notice (3-4 days prior to meeting date)
- Include Google Meet conference details automatically in meeting description
- Set RSVP requirements for tracking attendance confirmation
