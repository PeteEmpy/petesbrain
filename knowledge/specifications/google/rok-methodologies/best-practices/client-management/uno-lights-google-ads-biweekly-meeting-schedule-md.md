## Full Content

- Schedule regular bi-weekly touchpoints with Google Ads representatives for active accounts
- Use collaborative notes document (Google Docs) for structured knowledge capture during meetings
- Document action items and follow-ups in shared notes for accountability
- Maintain consistent meeting cadence to stay updated on platform changes and optimization opportunities
