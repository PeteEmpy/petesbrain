## Full Content

- Use Product Hero Labelizer as foundational segmentation across all e-commerce clients
- Allocate maximum budget and priority to Heroes for proven revenue generation
- Increase visibility for Sidekicks with lower initial ROAS targets to boost volume
- Isolate Villains in low-budget campaigns or exclude to prevent budget drain
- Test Zombies in controlled batches to discover hidden gems
- Create separate PMax campaigns or asset groups based on classification
- Monitor daily automated classification updates
- Investigate WHY Villains don't convert (pricing, product-market fit, listing quality)
- Use classification to guide Google's algorithm toward high-performing products
- Redirect budget from underperformers to proven winners
- Track products moving between classifications (Sidekicks becoming Heroes)
- Consider seasonality when evaluating Zombie products
