## Full Content

- Confirm meeting times explicitly via email before scheduled calls
- Remove duplicate or outdated recurring calendar invites to prevent confusion
- Centralize calendar management using single scheduling system
- Clean up calendar systems regularly to avoid conflicting invites
- Send meeting reminders 24 hours and 1 hour before scheduled time

- Reference specific client names in meeting invites and communications
