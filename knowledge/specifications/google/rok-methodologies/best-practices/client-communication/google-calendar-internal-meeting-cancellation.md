## Summary

- Document cancellation reasons when provided to maintain context for future interactions
- Confirm whether rescheduling is needed or if issues were resolved through alternative channels
- Archive administrative-only records with limited actionable value to maintain clean knowledge base
- Maintain contact log for Google partnership touchpoints regardless of meeting outcome
