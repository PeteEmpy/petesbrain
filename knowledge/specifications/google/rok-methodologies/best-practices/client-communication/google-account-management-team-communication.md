## Summary

- Maintain multiple contact points with Google account team (primary strategist and manager)
- Know the escalation path for urgent matters when primary contact unavailable
- Distinguish between strategic account matters (require strategist/manager) and technical support issues (A+ Team)
- Document contact changes and interim arrangements for client communication continuity
- Use 24/7 A+ Team for immediate technical issues: billing, policy, Merchant Centre, Analytics
