# British vs American English Reference

Quick reference for consistent spelling across UK and US clients.

---

## When to Use Which

**British English**: All UK clients (default)
- Devonshire Hotels
- Smythson UK
- National Design Academy
- Tree2mydoor
- Go Glean
- Crowd Control
- Godshot
- Bright Minds
- Uno Lighting
- Grain Guard
- Clear Prospects

**American English**: US clients or US-specific campaigns
- Superspace US campaigns
- Smythson US campaigns
- Any client with ".com" US website

## ROK Style Guide

Regardless of British or American English:

**ROAS Format**: Always percentage
- ✅ 400%, 292%, 550%
- ❌ £4.00, £2.92, £5.50

**Currency**: Match client's market
- UK: £, GBP
- US: $, USD

**Date Format**:
- Filenames: YYYY-MM-DD (2025-11-07)
- Written: [Month] [Day], [Year] (November 7, 2025)

**Numbers**:
- Thousands: Comma separator (1,000 not 1.000)
- Decimals: Period (1.5 not 1,5)
- Currency: Two decimal places (£1,234.56)
- Percentages: No decimal for whole numbers (400% not 400.0%)
