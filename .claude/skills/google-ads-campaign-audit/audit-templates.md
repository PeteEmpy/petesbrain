## Template Selection Guide

| Scenario | Use Template |
|----------|--------------|
| Weekly check-in | Template 1: Weekly Performance |
| Month-end client report | Template 2: Month-End Report |
| Budget discussion | Template 3: Impression Share |
| Shopping campaign focus | Template 4: Product Deep Dive |
| New client onboarding | Template 5: New Account Audit |
| Performance issue diagnosis | Mix of templates as needed |

## Customization Tips

1. **Adjust thresholds** based on client industry and margins
2. **Add client-specific sections** (e.g., seasonal factors, known issues)
3. **Include context** from previous audits or conversations
4. **Quantify impact** whenever possible ($XXX saved, XX% increase)
5. **Use emojis sparingly** for status indicators only
6. **Link to previous recommendations** and track implementation
