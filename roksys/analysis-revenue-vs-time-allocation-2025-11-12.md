**Time Allocation Data**:

*Task Time*:
- Source: Google Tasks active tasks (as of 2025-11-12)
- Time estimates extracted from task titles/notes
- Default: 1 hour per task if no estimate provided
- Total: 18.9 hours across 27 tasks

*Meeting Time*:
- Source: Granola meeting imports in `clients/[client]/meeting-notes/` (last 30 days)
- Script: `/Users/administrator/Documents/PetesBrain/shared/scripts/analyze_meeting_time.py`
- Duration estimation method:
  1. Check frontmatter metadata for explicit `duration` field (preferred)
  2. If not available, estimate from transcript word count using conservative bucketing:
     - >5000 words = 2.0 hours (very long meeting)
     - >3000 words = 1.5 hours (long meeting)
     - >1500 words = 1.0 hours (medium meeting)
     - >800 words = 0.75 hours (short meeting)
     - >300 words = 0.5 hours (very short meeting)
     - <300 words = 0.5 hours (default)
- Total: 32.0 hours across 22 meetings
- **Important**: Only counts transcript content AFTER frontmatter; does NOT search content for duration mentions (to avoid false matches like "12 to 24 hours" â†’ "1224 hours")

*Combined*:
- Total combined hours: 50.9 hours (18.9 task + 32.0 meeting)
- **Meeting time = 63% of total time allocation** - this was completely missing from task-only analysis!
- **Limitation**: Neither measure captures actual time spent, only estimated/planned work

**Variance Calculation**:
- Variance = (Time % - Revenue %)
- Positive variance = over-allocated (more time than revenue justifies)
- Negative variance = under-allocated (less time than revenue justifies)

**Thresholds**:
- âœ… Well-balanced: Â±2% variance
- âš ï¸ Attention needed: Â±2-5% variance
