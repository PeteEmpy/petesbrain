## OpenAI launches ChatGPT Atlas browser

#### üö® **Our Report**  

**OpenAI has released ChatGPT Atlas, a web browser with ChatGPT built directly into it. Atlas lets users browse, search, and complete tasks without leaving the page.** It integrates ChatGPT‚Äôs memory, agent mode, and privacy settings so users can automate actions, summarize pages, and manage browsing data in one place.

#### üîì Key Points 

* **Atlas lets ChatGPT within your browser**, performing tasks like research, scheduling, or online shopping through agent mode, which is in preview for Plus, Pro, and Business users.

* **Browser memories allow ChatGPT to recall context from sites you visit**, helping it generate more relevant answers or continue projects later. Users can view, archive, or delete these memories anytime.

* **OpenAI says Atlas prioritizes user control and safety**, with safeguards that prevent the agent from running code, accessing local files, or acting on sensitive sites without confirmation.

**This launch signals OpenAI‚Äôs move into the browser market, positioning ChatGPT as a workspace that blends browsing and automation. **For enterprises, it points to a shift toward agentic AI systems capable of handling real workflows directly in the browser, raising both efficiency potential and governance questions around data visibility and automation risk.
