## Summary

- Bain & Company released 'The Southeast Asia CEO's Guide to AI Transformation' highlighting that organizations in the region are stuck in early AI testing phases
- Main issue identified: companies treat AI as a toolkit rather than a fundamental business transformation driver
- Report emphasizes that successful AI adoption requires reimagining how the business operates, not just implementing tools
- Bain opened a Singapore hub alongside the report, signaling commitment to regional AI advisory services
- Targets C-suite executives with strategic guidance on moving beyond pilot projects to enterprise-wide AI integration

## Key Insights

- Many organizations fail at AI adoption because they focus on tools rather than business model transformation - this applies to marketing departments seeking AI implementation
- The 'pilot purgatory' problem is real: testing AI features without strategic integration leads to stagnation across industries including marketing operations
- Executive-level buy-in and strategic vision (not just tactical implementation) is critical for successful AI transformation in any business function
- Regional focus on Southeast Asia suggests varying AI maturity levels across markets - important context for global marketing teams
