## Summary

- Organizations face increasing pressure to deploy AI solutions but are hindered by poor data quality
- Flawed data leads to unreliable AI insights and outputs, undermining business value
- Business leaders recognize the need for comprehensive data strategy overhauls before successful AI implementation
- Data quality issues represent a critical bottleneck for marketing AI adoption and automation initiatives

## Key Insights

- Data quality is a prerequisite for AI success - organizations must address data infrastructure issues before scaling AI initiatives
- The rush to adopt AI without proper data foundations creates a 'garbage in, garbage out' scenario that wastes resources and erodes trust in AI systems
- Marketing leaders should prioritize data governance, cleansing, and standardization as foundational elements of their AI strategy
- Organizations need to audit existing data quality, establish data quality metrics, and implement ongoing data hygiene practices before investing heavily in AI tools
