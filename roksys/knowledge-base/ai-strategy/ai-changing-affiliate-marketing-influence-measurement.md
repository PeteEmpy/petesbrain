## Summary

- AI is fundamentally shifting where and when influence occurs in affiliate marketing, moving discovery upstream in the customer journey
- Traditional click-based attribution models are becoming obsolete as AI changes how consumers interact with affiliate content
- Marketers need new measurement frameworks to track value and performance before the click happens
- Understanding AI-driven discovery patterns is crucial for adapting affiliate marketing strategies
- The transformation requires rethinking traditional affiliate marketing KPIs and performance metrics

## Key Insights

- AI is disrupting traditional click-based affiliate tracking by influencing purchase decisions earlier in the discovery phase, requiring marketers to develop pre-click measurement strategies
- The shift from transactional to informational influence means affiliate partners need to be evaluated on their ability to shape awareness and consideration, not just final conversions
- Marketers must invest in new attribution technologies and frameworks that can track AI-mediated interactions and assign value to upstream influence points
- Success in AI-era affiliate marketing requires understanding how AI aggregates and presents information from multiple sources before users ever click through to brand sites
