## Summary

- Google is testing Journey Aware Bidding, a new Smart Bidding model that optimizes Search campaigns using signals from the entire customer journey
- This represents an evolution in Google's Smart Bidding capabilities, moving beyond single-touchpoint optimization to full-funnel awareness
- The feature aims to drive smarter and more efficient Search campaign optimization by understanding user behavior across multiple interactions
- Currently in testing phase, this could significantly impact how Search campaigns are optimized and bid strategies are implemented

## Key Insights

- Journey Aware Bidding marks a shift from isolated conversion optimization to holistic customer journey optimization in Search campaigns
- Advertisers should prepare for increased automation that considers multiple touchpoints rather than last-click attribution
- This feature likely requires sufficient conversion data across the funnel to function effectively
- May impact campaign structure recommendations as Google moves toward more journey-based optimization
- Advertisers should monitor this rollout and test early if given access to understand impact on CPAs and conversion rates
