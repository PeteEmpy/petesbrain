## Summary

- Many clients initially focus on surface-level metrics like CTR and clicks
- True advertising success requires tracking meaningful conversions
- Businesses should prioritize bottom-of-funnel metrics that demonstrate real business impact

## Key Insights

- Vanity metrics like impressions and clicks do not indicate genuine marketing success
- Conversion tracking is critical for understanding actual business performance from ad campaigns
- Most businesses want tangible results (sign-ups, calls, emails) rather than mere awareness
