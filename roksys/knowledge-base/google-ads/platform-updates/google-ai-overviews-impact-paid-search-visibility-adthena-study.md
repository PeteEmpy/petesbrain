## Summary

- Adthena analyzed 21 million SERPs over two four-week periods to quantify the impact of Google's AI Overviews on paid search visibility
- AI Overviews are causing measurable decreases in paid search ad visibility across search engine results pages
- Different industries are experiencing varying levels of impact, with some sectors being affected earlier and more significantly than others
- The rollout of AI Overviews represents an accelerating shift in how paid search ads appear and perform on Google
- Data-driven analysis reveals specific patterns in where and how visibility is declining on SERPs

## Key Insights

- AI Overviews are fundamentally changing SERP layouts and reducing available real estate for paid search ads, requiring immediate strategic adjustments
- Industry-specific impact varies significantly - advertisers should analyze their specific vertical's exposure to AI Overview rollout
- Historical SERP positioning strategies may no longer be effective; advertisers need to adapt bidding and creative strategies to maintain visibility in AI Overview-dominated SERPs
- The 21 million SERP sample provides concrete evidence that AI Overviews are not a theoretical concern but an active challenge affecting current campaign performance
- Monitoring SERP visibility trends and share of voice metrics becomes critical for identifying when AI Overviews impact specific keyword sets or industries
