## Summary

- Google launches new Video assets page in Merchant Center for centralized video management
- Feature allows businesses to view, manage, and distribute videos across Google's advertising and organic surfaces
- AI-powered functionality connects video content directly to product feeds automatically
- Videos can be sourced directly from website and social channels with automated management
- Seamless distribution during ad creation with control over where videos appear across Google

## Key Insights

- Google is centralizing video asset management to reduce friction in cross-platform distribution
- AI integration automatically maps video content to product feeds, reducing manual work
- This update positions video as a first-class citizen in Google's e-commerce advertising ecosystem
- Businesses can now leverage existing social and website video content more efficiently across Google properties
