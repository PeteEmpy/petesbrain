## Summary

- Google Merchant Center introduces new Video assets page for centralized video management across advertising and organic surfaces
- AI-powered feature automatically connects video content to product feeds and enables seamless distribution across Google properties
- Merchants can now search, filter, and organize videos from websites and social channels in one unified interface
- Videos can be automatically leveraged during ad creation with controls for placement across Google's network
- Integration uses Google AI to drive customer engagement by linking video content directly to product data

## Key Insights

- This represents a significant platform update that bridges the gap between merchant video content and Google's advertising ecosystem, reducing manual effort in video campaign management
- The AI-driven connection between videos and product feeds suggests Google is moving toward more automated, content-rich shopping experiences that could improve conversion rates
- Centralized video management across both paid and organic surfaces indicates Google's push for unified commerce experiences, potentially giving merchants with video content a competitive advantage
- Actionable recommendation: Audit existing video content from website and social channels to ensure it's accessible for Google's automated detection and linking
- Actionable recommendation: Test the new Video assets page to understand how AI connects videos to products, and optimize video metadata/content to improve AI matching accuracy
