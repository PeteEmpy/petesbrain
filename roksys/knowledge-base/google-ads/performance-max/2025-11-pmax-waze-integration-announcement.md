## Summary

- Google has integrated Waze as a new placement option for Performance Max campaigns, expanding reach to drivers using the navigation app
- The update provides advertisers with additional inventory to reach users during their driving journeys with location-based advertising
- Enhanced performance visibility features have been added to PMax campaigns alongside the Waze integration
- This expansion represents Google's continued investment in Performance Max automation and cross-platform reach

## Key Insights

- Waze integration opens new opportunities for local businesses and drive-to-store campaigns to reach consumers actively navigating
- Advertisers should monitor Waze placement performance separately to understand its contribution to overall PMax campaign results
- The improved performance visibility features suggest Google is responding to advertiser demands for more transparency in automated campaigns
- Consider testing PMax campaigns with location assets and store visit goals to maximize the value of Waze placements
- This update reinforces the trend toward consolidated campaign types with broader automated placement options
