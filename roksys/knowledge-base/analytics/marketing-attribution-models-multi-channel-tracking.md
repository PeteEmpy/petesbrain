## Summary

- Marketing attribution helps identify which channels and touchpoints actually drive conversions, eliminating guesswork from marketing budget allocation
- Modern marketing requires tracking multiple customer touchpoints across various channels to understand the complete customer journey
- Digital marketing attribution provides data-driven insights to optimize marketing spend and improve ROI by showing what works and what doesn't
- Without proper attribution, businesses waste budget on ineffective channels while underinvesting in high-performing ones

## Key Insights

- Attribution is essential for understanding how different marketing channels work together to drive conversions, particularly important for Google Ads practitioners who need to see how paid search fits into the broader customer journey
- Proper attribution models help eliminate wasted ad spend by identifying which touchpoints genuinely contribute to conversions versus those that receive credit without impact
- Cross-channel attribution is critical in today's complex marketing landscape where customers interact with multiple touchpoints before converting
