## Summary

- CMOs are experiencing a critical 'make-or-break moment' as traditional digital marketing channels show signs of collapse
- Personalization strategies that marketers have relied on are backfiring and losing effectiveness
- Gartner research indicates CMOs must fundamentally rethink their marketing approaches or face irrelevance
- The crisis affects core digital marketing pillars including channel management and customer personalization
- This represents a strategic inflection point requiring significant changes to marketing technology and methodology

## Key Insights

- Traditional digital marketing channels are proving unsustainable, forcing a fundamental reassessment of channel strategy and investment allocation
- Over-reliance on personalization has created consumer fatigue and privacy concerns, requiring a new balance between personalized and contextual marketing
- CMOs need to evaluate AI-powered alternatives and new technologies to replace failing traditional digital tactics
- This industry-wide crisis creates both risk for those slow to adapt and opportunity for early adopters of next-generation marketing approaches
- Marketing leaders should conduct immediate audits of their digital channel performance and personalization effectiveness to identify vulnerabilities
