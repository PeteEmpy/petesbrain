## Trump launches U.S. Tech Force

* The Trump administration launched the U.S. Tech Force, a government initiative that will **hire 1,000 engineers and specialists for two-year terms** to work on AI infrastructure and technology projects across federal agencies.

* The program partners with major tech companies, including AWS, Apple, Google, Microsoft, Nvidia, OpenAI, Oracle, and Salesforce. **Participants can transition to full-time roles** after completing their government service.

* **Annual salaries will range from $150,000 to $200,000**, positioning the program as a competitive pathway between public and private sector AI work as the U.S. aims to maintain technological leadership against China.
