## How AI cut content costs for a social app startup

* **Biz4Group**, a software company specializing in AI solutions, built a social media app that let users create and share AI-generated visuals.

* The startup faced **high token expenses** from repeated AI requests for similar content, threatening its budget and long-term scalability.

* Biz4Group introduced a **caching system** that stored generated media, reusing it instead of triggering AI calls for identical prompts.

* This approach **reduced token usage and boosted engagement**, with user-generated content up 50% and session time increasing by 40%.
