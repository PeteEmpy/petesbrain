# National Design Academy - Campaign Naming Convention

**Date**: 2025-11-05
**Client**: National Design Academy
**Category**: Campaign Structure

## Key Information

The NDA Google Ads account comprises both UK and international campaigns.

**Identification Method**:
- **UK Campaigns**: Identified by "| UK" in the campaign name
- **International Campaigns**: All campaigns WITHOUT "| UK" in the name

This naming convention is critical for:
- Budget allocation by geography
- Performance reporting (UK vs International)
- ROAS analysis by market
- Geographic strategy decisions

## Context

This applies to all campaign types (Performance Max, Search, etc.) and is essential when analyzing account performance or making strategic recommendations.
