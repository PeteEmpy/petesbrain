## Available Case Studies

### Google Ads Optimization

#### [Target ROAS Optimization - Bright Minds (2025)](target-roas-optimization-bright-minds-2025.md)
**Status**: ✅ Proven Success
**Result**: +48% conversions, 632% ROAS maintained
**Replication**: HIGH confidence

**Summary**: Reduced unrealistic Target ROAS (800% → 550%) on brand search campaign, allowing Google's algorithm to capture more volume while maintaining strong profitability.

**Key Learning**: Unrealistic Target ROAS settings restrict volume. Set targets at or slightly above actual performance levels.

**Applicable to**:
- Brand search campaigns
- Campaigns with target >200pp above actual ROAS
- Impression share <90%
- Volume scaling strategies

## Contributing New Case Studies

When documenting a new case study, include:

### Required Sections
- **Executive Summary** - Quick overview of results
- **Problem Statement** - What issue was being addressed
- **Analysis Process** - How you identified the solution
- **Implementation** - What you did and when
- **Results** - Quantitative performance data
- **Success Factors** - Why it worked
- **Lessons Learned** - Key takeaways
- **Replication Guide** - Step-by-step for others to follow

### Optional Sections
- Risk mitigation strategies
- Client communication examples
- Financial impact analysis
- Related resources
- Future applications

### Template

Use the Bright Minds Target ROAS case study as a template for structure and level of detail.
