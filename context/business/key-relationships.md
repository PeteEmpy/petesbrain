## Customer Segments

### Primary: E-commerce SMEs (£500K - £5M revenue)

**Profile:**
- Growing e-commerce companies (online stores)
- Founders or marketing directors (decision-makers)
- £500K - £5M annual revenue (sweet spot for profitable PPC)
- Looking to grow paid search revenue efficiently

**Examples:**
- Superspace, Tree2mydoor, Just Bin Bags, Positive Bakes, Uno Lighting

**Pain Points:**
- Frustrated with agency churn (juniors leaving, account continuity lost)
- Overwhelmed by complexity of Google Ads management
- Feeling like they're not getting expert-level service
- Unclear on ROAS and true cost of acquisition
- Need strategic partner, not just operator

**What They Value:**
- Owner access (not junior gatekeepers)
- Deep optimisation and strategy
- ROAS improvement (bottom line impact)
- Understandable reporting
- Responsive, proactive service

---

### Secondary: E-commerce Enterprise (£5M+ revenue)

**Profile:**
- Established e-commerce brands
- Multiple revenue streams
- Complex campaign structures (multi-region, multi-product, multi-channel)
- High-stakes optimisation (£1M+ annual PPC spend)
- Senior marketing leadership

**Examples:**
- Smythson (luxury leather goods), Devonshire Hotels, National Design Academy

**Pain Points:**
- Need strategic partner with business acumen (not just tactical)
- Managing complex multi-region campaigns
- High value decisions (every 1% ROAS change = £50K+)
- Finding consultant experienced enough for scale
- Needing daily oversight on major accounts

**What They Value:**
- 22+ years of expertise and credibility
- Business acumen (understands their metrics, margins, strategy)
- Daily monitoring and proactive optimisation
- Strategic insights on market expansion
- Ability to manage complexity

---

### Tertiary: Multi-Location Service Businesses

**Profile:**
- Services with local/regional presence
- Multiple brand or location structures
- Complex reporting requirements (by location, by brand)
- Need consolidated strategy across entities

**Example:**
- Clear Prospects (3 wellness brands)

**Pain Points:**
- Consolidating reporting across multiple accounts
- Brand-specific strategy and optimisation
- Separate P&L per location/brand
- Complexity of multi-account management
