## Usage Instructions

When working on a specific client in Claude.ai:

1. Copy the relevant Google Doc URL from above
2. Paste it into Claude.ai with: "Reference this document for [Client Name] context: [URL]"
3. Claude.ai will read the document and incorporate the context into its responses

All documents are automatically synced from Pete's Brain CONTEXT.md files every morning at 7 AM GMT.
