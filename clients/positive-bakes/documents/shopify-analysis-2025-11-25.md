## Attribution Analysis

### Google Ads Share: 20.2%

**What's driving the other 79.8%?**

Likely sources (in order of typical contribution):
1. **Direct Traffic** (~30-40%)
   - Repeat customers
   - Brand awareness from Google Ads
   - Email recipients typing URL directly

2. **Organic Search** (~15-25%)
   - SEO performance
   - Brand searches
   - Product searches

3. **Email Marketing** (~10-20%)
   - Klaviyo campaigns
   - Cart abandonment flows
   - Newsletter subscribers

4. **Social Media** (~5-10%)
   - Instagram/Facebook organic
   - Influencer/word-of-mouth

5. **Referral Traffic** (~5-10%)
   - Partner sites
   - Blog mentions
   - Affiliate links

**Note:** These are estimates based on typical e-commerce attribution. Actual breakdown available in Google Analytics 4.

### Google Ads Halo Effect

Google Ads likely contributing to non-paid channels:
- Brand awareness driving direct traffic
- Product discovery leading to later organic searches
- Retargeting reminders triggering email engagement
- Overall visibility increasing all channels

**True Google Ads impact likely higher than 20.2% when accounting for assisted conversions.**
