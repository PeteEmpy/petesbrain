## Key Learnings & Insights

### Critical: Conversion Lag Effect (Nov 2025)

**âš ï¸ ALWAYS account for 1-2 day conversion lag when analyzing recent performance:**

- **Nov 11-12 Analysis (Nov 13, 2025)**: Account showed apparent performance decline when analyzed by click date
  - Click date ROAS: 116-165% (appeared to be failing)
  - Conversion date ROAS: 172-234% (actual performance was strong)
  - Conversion lag: 26-66% of conversions come 1-2 days after click

- **Impact on Analysis**:
  - Recent 2-3 days ALWAYS look artificially worse than reality
  - Conversion rate appears to drop when it's actually stable or improving
  - ROAS appears to decline when it's actually meeting/exceeding targets

- **Correct Analysis Method**:
  - Use `conversions_by_conversion_date` metric (not `conversions`)
  - Use `conversions_value_by_conversion_date` (not `conversions_value`)
  - Allow 2-3 days for data to mature before drawing conclusions
  - Compare week-over-week using conversion date attribution

- **Example**: Nov 6-12 performance
  - By click date: 1.64% CR, 137% ROAS âŒ (FALSE - data not mature)
  - By conversion date: 2.30% CR, 224% ROAS âœ… (TRUE - actual performance)

**This is NOT a seasonal anomaly - this is the normal attribution pattern for this account.**
