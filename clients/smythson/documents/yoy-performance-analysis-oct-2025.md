## The Conversion Rate Story

### Why Conversion Rates Dropped 49% YoY

**Industry Context (2024-2025 changes):**
- iOS 17/18 privacy updates (stricter tracking prevention)
- Google Consent Mode v2 enforcement (March 2024)
- Third-party cookie deprecation testing
- Safari ITP (Intelligent Tracking Prevention) improvements
- Chrome Privacy Sandbox rollout

**Impact on Smythson:**
- 2024 Oct-Nov: 7-9% conversion rate (full tracking)
- 2025 Oct-Nov: 4% conversion rate (degraded tracking)
- **This is NOT a performance issue - this is a measurement issue**

**What this means:**
- Many conversions happening but not being tracked
- Smart Bidding optimizing with partial data
- ROAS calculations are understated (actual ROAS likely higher)
