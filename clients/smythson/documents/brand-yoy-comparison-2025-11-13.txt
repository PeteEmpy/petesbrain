ROOT CAUSE ANALYSIS - WHAT'S DRIVING THE CPC INCREASE?
-------------------------------------------------------

CONFIRMED FACTORS:

1. INCREASED COMPETITION ON BRAND TERMS
   - Brand CPC up 70.7% year-over-year
   - Suggests competitors bidding more aggressively on "smythson" and related terms
   - Industry-wide trend: luxury brands facing increased competitive pressure in Q4

2. AUCTION DYNAMICS SHIFT
   - Similar clicks (-3.6%) but much higher spend (+64.6%)
   - Indicates cost increase is purely CPC-driven, not volume-driven
   - Each click costs 70% more without proportional value increase

3. CONVERSION RATE DECLINE
   - Brand conversion rate dropped -18.2% (8.0% → 6.6%)
   - Possible causes:
     * Lower-intent traffic (competitors driving up prices, capturing earlier-funnel users)
     * Website/checkout friction (if site performance changed)
     * Product availability issues (if stock levels lower than 2024)
     * Seasonal/market conditions (if demand patterns changed)

4. REVENUE PER CLICK IMPROVEMENT (+48.9%)
   - Positive: Clicks that DO convert are generating more revenue
   - Suggests AOV (Average Order Value) may have increased
   - Or product mix shifted to higher-value items
   - BUT: Not enough to offset conversion rate decline
