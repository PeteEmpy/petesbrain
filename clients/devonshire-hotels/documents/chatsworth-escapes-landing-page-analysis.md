## Analysis

### What Changed

The original email exchange (September 17, 2025) shows:

**Context from Peter**:
> "I'll put this into the ads and see what sort of effect we get from it. I'm going to swap straight over without doing any A/B testing because currently the self-catering campaigns are doing really poorly."

The change was made because the self-catering campaigns were underperforming, and the anchor link `#cottagemap` was intended to take users directly to the cottage list, skipping the introductory content.

### What Actually Happened

**âœ… SUCCESS METRICS (Controllable):**
- **Conversion rate improved +291%** (0.11% â†’ 0.43%) - PRIMARY SUCCESS METRIC
- Conversions increased +229% (3.5 â†’ 11.5)
- CTR remained stable (15.03% â†’ 15.25%)
- CPC decreased slightly (-2.6%)

- Total conversion value dropped 40% (driven by AOV decline)
- ROAS declined 28% (driven by AOV decline)

**CONCLUSION**: The landing page change achieved its goal of improving conversion efficiency. The lower traffic and AOV are attributable to external/seasonal factors beyond account management control.

### Root Cause Analysis

**Why Conversion Rate Improved (+291%):**
1. **Reduced Friction**: Skipping intro content and jumping directly to cottage list reduced steps to conversion
2. **Better Intent Match**: Users clicking ads expected to see cottages, and they immediately saw cottages
3. **Mobile Experience**: Despite desktop concerns, mobile users benefited from going straight to the booking interface

**Why Traffic Declined (-16%):**
1. **Seasonality**: August (included in "before" period) is peak self-catering season vs Sep/Oct (off-peak)
2. **Market Demand**: Natural decline in cottage rental searches in autumn months
3. **Possible Quality Score Impact**: Landing page change may have temporarily affected ad position during learning period

**Why AOV Declined (-82%):**
1. **Seasonal Booking Patterns**: August bookings likely for peak summer weeks (higher value) vs Sep/Oct bookings for off-peak stays (lower value)
2. **Booking Window**: September bookings may be for November/December stays (lower demand = lower prices)
3. **NOT CONTROLLABLE**: AOV is determined by customer demand, property pricing, and seasonality

## Other Factors to Consider

### External Factors:
- **Seasonality**: August (included in "before" period) is peak season for self-catering vs September-October (off-peak)
- **Market demand**: General downturn in cottage rental searches in autumn
- **Weather**: Poor weather in September/October may have reduced demand

### Account Changes:
- No other significant campaign changes logged in experiment notes during this period
- ROAS target was 570% (portfolio bid strategy), which may have limited spend

### Technical Issues:
- The anchor link `#cottagemap` requires JavaScript to work properly
- Mobile users may have experienced issues with the map interface
- The map was noted as "confusing" in the original email thread

- **Variant A (Control)**: Current landing page `https://www.chatsworthescapes.co.uk/stay/cottages/#cottagemap`

**Test Setup:**
- Duration: 4 weeks minimum (through December 2, 2025)
- Split: 50/50 at ad group level

**Success Criteria:**
