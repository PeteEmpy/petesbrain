## Business Context

### Product/Service Details

**Just Bin Bags** is a UK-based family-run business specializing in polythene solutions, bin bags, and disposable products.

**Business Model**: E-commerce retailer offering wide range of polythene products at competitive prices, serving both B2C and B2B markets.

**Core Products**: Bin bags and refuse sacks

**Product Categories**:
- **Bin Bags** - Various sizes, thicknesses, and types of bin bags on rolls
- **Bin Liners** - Wheelie bin liners in multiple sizes
- **Compactor Sacks** - Heavy-duty black compactor sacks
- **Rubble Sacks** - For broken glass, rubble, construction waste
- **Shrink Wrap** - Black shrink wrap for pallets and packaging
- **Pallet Hoods** - Bespoke pallet hoods available
- **Dust Extractor Sacks** - Clear dust extractor sacks
- **Medical/Care** - Healthcare disposables including gloves, face masks, protective wear, hygiene supplies

**Multi-Brand Structure**:
- **JBB** (Just Bin Bags): Main brand - "Quality prices for rubbish bags"
- **JHD** (Just Health Disposals): Sub-brand focusing on healthcare disposables and medical supplies

**USP**:
- **"Quality prices for rubbish bags"** - Competitive pricing positioning
- **Family-run business** - Personal touch, excellent customer service focus
- **Top quality products** - Emphasis on product quality despite competitive pricing
- **Wide range** - All types of polythene solutions in one place
- **B2B capabilities** - Can supply from single boxes to full container loads
- **Healthcare expansion** - Diversified into disposable gloves, face masks, protective wear for healthcare providers

**Target Market**: 
- **Primary**: B2B - Businesses, healthcare providers, warehouses, construction companies needing bulk polythene products
- **Secondary**: B2C - Homeowners needing bin bags, bin liners, garden supplies
- **Demographics**: Value-conscious consumers and businesses seeking quality at competitive prices
