## üí≥ Payment Methods

### UK Enrollments
- **BACS**: Primary method (majority of students)
- **Secure Trading**: Secondary payment processor
- **GoCardless**: Instalment option for payment plans
- **Checkout**: Alternative gateway
- **Loans**: Available financing option

### International Enrollments
- **Overseas BACS**: Primary method (most international students)
- **Checkout**: Secondary method
- **Voucher**: Less common but available

**Insight**: International students predominantly use BACS transfers, suggesting high payment value and commitment level.
