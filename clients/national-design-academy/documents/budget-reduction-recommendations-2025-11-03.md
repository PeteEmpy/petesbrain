## Risk Mitigation

### Potential Risks

**1. Loss of International Reach**
- Pausing 10 international campaigns may reduce brand presence
- Mitigation: Focus remaining budget on proven markets (UAE, India, Europe, USA/Canada)

**2. High Performers Hit Budget Ceiling**
- Budget increases may not produce proportional conversion increases
- Mitigation: Monitor ROAS daily for first 2 weeks, adjust if diminishing returns appear

**3. Seasonal Variations**
- September is traditionally high-enrollment month
- Mitigation: Re-evaluate in November (after September spike)

### Success Metrics

Track these KPIs weekly:
1. **Total Conversions:** Target 180+/month (current 189/month)
2. **CPA:** Target <£170 (current £190)
3. **UK Conversion Share:** Target 75%+ (current 70%)
4. **ROAS:** Target >500% (current 515% international, higher UK)
5. **Spend Compliance:** Stay within £30,000/month budget
