## Key Findings

### ✅ Positive Outcomes

1. **Spend Reduction Achieved**
   - Daily spend dropped from £1,130 to £765 (-32%)
   - On track for target monthly spend of ~£23,000-£25,000
   - Down from October's £40,000 overspend

2. **Efficiency Improved**
   - Cost per conversion improved from £212 to £191 (-10%)
   - Demonstrates that cutting underperforming campaigns improved overall efficiency
   - Nov 7 showed exceptional performance: 16.5 conversions at £48 CPA

3. **Conversion Quality Maintained**
   - Still generating 3-4 conversions per day on average
   - Nov 7 spike (16.5 conversions) suggests strong campaign performance potential
   - No collapse in lead volume despite 32% spend reduction

### ⚠️ Observations

1. **Conversion Volatility**
   - High daily variance (0-16.5 conversions per day)
   - This is typical for lead gen accounts with longer conversion cycles
   - Need 2-3 weeks of data to assess true impact

2. **Budget Implementation Timing**
   - Nov 5 still showed high spend (£1,269) - budget changes may have taken effect mid-day
   - Nov 6 also elevated (£1,128) - campaigns may still have been spending previous day's budgets
   - Nov 7-10 show more stable lower spend levels (£446-£997)

3. **Weekend Impact**
   - Nov 9-10 includes weekend data which may have different patterns
   - Need full week of weekday data for proper comparison
