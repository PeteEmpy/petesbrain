- **ROAS Compression September 2025**: ROAS declined 6.9% MoM (1.48x‚Üí1.38x), more severe than 2024's pattern
- **October 26-28 Click Spike**: Campaign "T2MD | P Max | HP&P Roses" experienced 316% click increase (226 ‚Üí 2,630 clicks/day)
  - **Root Cause Identified (Oct 29)**: Product 01090 (The Olive Tree Gift - ¬£28.99, 2L pot) was flickering in/out of Google Merchant Center feed multiple times per day
  - **Timeline**: Product removed and re-added Oct 25, 26, 27, 28; on Oct 29 alone flickered 4 times (removed/added/removed/added)
  - **Why This Caused Spike**: Google's algorithm treats repeatedly re-added products as "new inventory" each time, giving them auction priority
  - **Impact**: Oct 28 peak - 1,465 clicks (56% of all campaign clicks), up from 8-30 clicks/day baseline; CPC dropped 76% (¬£0.50 ‚Üí ¬£0.12)
  - **Likely Cause**: Stock synchronization issues - product going in/out of stock rapidly in inventory system
  - **Client Response (Oct 30)**: Gareth confirmed high-profile press coverage for Olive Trees in large readership magazines during this period, driving direct traffic
  - **Combined Effect Analysis**: Press coverage likely created brand awareness ‚Üí increased branded/category searches ‚Üí higher CTR on paid ads when product appeared. The Merchant Center feed flickering amplified this by giving the product algorithmic priority at the exact moment demand was elevated. Result: Perfect storm of increased demand + algorithmic boost = massive click spike on paid ads.
  - **Strategic Learning**: Press coverage can significantly amplify paid search performance when product availability is stable. However, feed instability during high-demand periods can cause unpredictable cost spikes. Future press campaigns should be coordinated with enhanced feed monitoring to ensure stable inventory data.
  - **Current Status**: Product active at ¬£28.99 and performing well when stable
  - **Analysis doc**: `/clients/tree2mydoor/click-spike-analysis-oct-2025.md`
  - **Key Learning**: Feed instability causes severe algorithmic disruption. Need stable stock levels for high performers. Enhanced tracking now enabled to catch price/availability changes immediately.
