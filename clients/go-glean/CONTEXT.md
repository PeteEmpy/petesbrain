### **‚≠ê CORE STRATEGY: Product Hero Segmentation Framework**

**CRITICAL FOR AUDIT ANALYSIS**: Go Glean uses the **Product Hero label system** as its primary campaign segmentation strategy. This is NOT traditional product category segmentation.

**What This Means:**
- Campaigns are structured by **performance labels** (Heroes & Sidekicks, Zombies, Villains), not product types
- Product Hero automatically classifies products based on performance data and syncs labels to Google Merchant Center
- Campaign names "H&S&Z" and "Villains" refer to Product Hero performance tiers, not product categories
- This strategy is consistent across all Connor Heaps businesses (Go Glean, Grain Guard, Crowd Control)

**Analysis Implications:**
- Budget allocation should consider label-based performance patterns (Heroes typically outperform Villains)
- Campaign ROAS targets may differ by label tier (Heroes can sustain higher targets)
