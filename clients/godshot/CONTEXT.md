- **Impact**: Cannot reliably attribute revenue to specific Google Ads campaigns/products. Campaign optimization may be based on inaccurate signals.
- **Root Causes**:

  4. **Browse-and-discover behavior**: Customers click on discovery sets/samples in ads but purchase full-size products on site
