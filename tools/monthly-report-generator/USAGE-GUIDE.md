## Key Metrics Explained

- **ROAS**: Revenue √∑ Spend (e.g., 5.80x = ¬£5.80 revenue per ¬£1 spent)
- **Pacing**: (Actual Spend √∑ Budget) √ó 100 (e.g., 91.87% = under budget)
- **CTR**: (Clicks √∑ Impressions) √ó 100 (e.g., 10.07%)
- **CPA**: Spend √∑ Conversions (cost per booking)
