## üìä Pricing & Performance

### Haiku (Default - Economical)
- **Input**: $0.25 per 1M tokens
- **Output**: $1.25 per 1M tokens
- **Speed**: 15-20 seconds
- **Cost per generation**: ~$0.0015 (keywords)

### Sonnet (Premium Quality)
- **Input**: $3.00 per 1M tokens
- **Output**: $15.00 per 1M tokens
- **Speed**: 20-30 seconds
- **Cost per generation**: ~$0.0180 (keywords)

### Cost Comparison
- **Savings**: 12x cheaper with Haiku
- **Recommendation**: Use Haiku for most cases, Sonnet for critical campaigns

5. Use "ü§ñ Generate" buttons for AI assistance:

7. Submit (entity created in PAUSED state)
8. Review in Google Ads UI before enabling
